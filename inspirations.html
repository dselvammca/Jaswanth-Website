Inspirations & Role Models

People whose dedication and mindset inspire me to learn, practice, and stay humble.

Ravindra Jadeja (Cricket)

Electric on the field—an all‑rounder who proves consistency is a superpower. His work rate, fitness, and calm under pressure motivate me during cricket practice. I try to bring his energy and focus to school sports.

Lee Chong Wei (Badminton)

Legendary footwork, discipline, and never‑give‑up attitude. He teaches me to move smart, not just fast, and to respect opponents. Badminton is my favorite because of players like him.

Dr A. P. J. Abdul Kalam (Science & Leadership)

India’s “People’s President” and a scientist who loved teaching. His life shows how curiosity and kindness can live together. Science is for serving people—his biggest lesson.

Sundar Pichai (Technology Leadership)

From student to leading teams that build tools for billions—stay humble, keep learning, help others.

Viswanathan Anand (Chess)

Lightning‑fast thinking and sportsmanship. Chess teaches patience and planning—useful for debugging.

M. S. Dhoni (Calm Leadership)

Cool under pressure and a mentor to young players. Leadership is action, not a title.

Mary Kom (Grit)

Champion, mother, and fighter who never stops improving. Use challenges as fuel.
